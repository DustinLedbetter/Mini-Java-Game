Rules//////////////////////////////////////////////////
The Players can move up and down and shoot at each other.
The first Player to reach ten hits on his opponent wins.

Player 1//////////////////////////////////////////////
"w" = go up
"s" = go down
"space bar" = shoot

Player 2//////////////////////////////////////////////
"up arrow" = go up
"down arrow" = go down
"Enter" = shoot
